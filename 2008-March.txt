Welcome to the posps-devel mailing list!

This list is intended for discussion of the development of the posps
specification, tests and reference implementation. You can also post on any
other topics which you feel may be of interest for developers.

Postings to this list are stored in a searchable archive. Therefore, if you
post your questions or answers to the list, this archive will become a
knowledge base which will benefit all persons taking part in posps
development.
